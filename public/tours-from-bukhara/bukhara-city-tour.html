Tour Description

This tour offers you to see the best in Bukhara in one day. With the UNESCO world heritage site which encompasses the whole center of the city, Bukhara has preserved the ancient ambiance of mid-centuries. It is one of the best sites to feel the spirit and mood of the Muslim architecture in Uzbekistan

1

Bolo Hauz mosque

After the breakfast you will set out to visit the Bolo – Khauz mosque. The monument’s unique feature is the columns on the iwan (terrace) which is decorated with “muqarnas” a form of ornamented vaulting in Islamic architecture.

2

Ark fortress

From the mosque, you will head off to the Ark of Bukhara which was the military fortress and residence for Emirs of Bukhara until 1920.

3

Ismail Samani mausoleum

Mausoleum of Samanids is the shrine of Ismail Samani the founder of Samanid dynasty one of the last Persian dynasties ruled in Central Asia in 9-10th centuries

4

Chashma Ayub mausoleum

Chashma-Ayub Mausoleum is a 12th century monument connected with the legend of prophet Job striking the ground with his staff causing a spring to emerge.

5

Poi-Kalyan Ensemble

The one of main ensembles which is located in the center of Bukhara and with its the symbol of the old Bukhara – 47 meter minaret Kalyan.

6

Madrasah of Ulugbek

Ulugbek was one of the greatest astronomers and patron of the education so he ordered to build another madrasah in Bukhara.

7

Magoki-Attori Mosque

Magoki-Attori Mosque is one of the oldest mosques in Bukhara dating back to 9th century AD.

8

Lyabi-Khauz Ensemble

Lyabi-Khauz Ensemble is the historical center of Bukhara consisting of three buildings with a large pond in the middle.
